Professional guide

Career Development into engineering in high school

simple guide with what classes to take. This is ONLY Rockwall-Heath isd.

01

Freshmen year. Should I take Robotics I or Engineering Design Process?

This is a very simple question to answer. Its, Robotics I because insted of take mostly notes you get to build and test robots. With drones as well.

Also for robotics you don't need algebra, but if you are going down this path I would still recommend taking as much math as possible. so be in at least geomerty this year. If not taking it over the summer.
02

Sophomore year. Take Engineering Design Process

Take this class to develop your design and problem-solving skills for engineering.

Try to get involved in engineering competitions or clubs to apply what you learn in real-world scenarios. Take more math/science courses. so take on ramps
03

Junior year. 2 options: Wait or Now!

You will be in ap physics 1 this year (unless you took it last year). Now should you take Advanced Engineering design & Presentation or wait till senior year to take Practicum Engineering??

Since this should be in phyics by now focus on this. to help senior year this year you could take gov and eco this year to help senior year. If you are not in advanced math clsases take advanced engineering design & presentation. to help your chances into practicum engineering. again take onramps
04

senior year. Take Practicum Engineering

Now you are ready to take the practicum engineering course. you should have taken physics 1 by now and should get in easy, or have taken Advanced Engineering design & Presentation and should also get in easy.

This year will be your hardest year so far. due to taking AP physics 2, AP physics C: Mechanics, AP physics C: Electricity and magnetism, and practicum engineering.
